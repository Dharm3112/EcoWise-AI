# Recommended Project

## EcoWise AI: Smart Waste Segregation & Sustainability Assistant

### SDG Alignment

* **SDG 11:** Sustainable Cities and Communities
* **SDG 12:** Responsible Consumption and Production
* Secondary: **SDG 13 Climate Action**

---

## Problem Statement

Many people do not know how to properly dispose of waste. As a result:

* Recyclable waste ends up in landfills.
* E-waste is disposed of incorrectly.
* Municipal waste management becomes inefficient.
* Environmental pollution increases.

### Problem Statement Format

> How might we use AI to help citizens identify and segregate waste correctly so that cities can become more sustainable and environmentally friendly?

---

## Target Users

* Students
* Households
* Municipal workers
* Schools and colleges
* Smart city initiatives

---

## AI Solution Overview

Develop an AI-powered assistant where users can:

### Option 1: Image-Based Waste Identification

User uploads an image of waste.

Examples:

* Plastic bottle
* Battery
* Food waste
* Glass bottle
* Cardboard

AI identifies:

* Waste type
* Disposal category
* Recycling recommendations

---

### Option 2: Conversational Sustainability Assistant

User asks:

> "How should I dispose of a used battery?"

AI answers:

* Hazardous Waste
* Nearby e-waste collection suggestion
* Environmental impact information

---

### Option 3: Sustainability Insights

AI provides:

* Daily sustainability tips
* Recycling awareness
* Carbon footprint reduction suggestions

---

# AI Components Used

This aligns perfectly with internship guidelines.

### Prompt Engineering

Use prompts to classify waste and provide disposal guidance.

### IBM Granite / LLM

Use AI for:

* Classification

* Sustainability education

### RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

Store:

* Waste management rules
* Recycling policies
* Municipal guidelines

AI retrieves relevant information before responding.

# System Workflow

```text
User Input

    ↓
AI Classification
    ↓
Knowledge Base (RAG)
    ↓
Recommendation Engine
    ↓
Disposal Guidance
    ↓
Sustainability Impact Report
```

# Responsible AI Considerations

### Fairness

Provide recommendations independent of user background.

### Transparency

Explain why waste was classified in a specific category.

### Ethics

Avoid misleading disposal information.

### Privacy

Do not store user images permanently.
